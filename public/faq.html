Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about equipment borrowing and campus services.

Equipment Borrowing

How do I borrow equipment?

Log in to your account, go to "Book Equipment" from the dashboard, select the item you need, choose the date, and submit your request. An admin will approve it before you can pick it up.

How long can I borrow equipment?

Most equipment can be borrowed for up to 7 days. Some specialized items may have shorter loan periods. Check the item details when booking.

What happens if I return equipment late?

Late returns incur a fee of $5 per day. Repeated late returns may result in borrowing privileges being suspended.

Can I extend my borrowing period?

Extensions can be requested through the system up to 3 days before the due date. Extensions are subject to availability and admin approval.

Account & Login

How do I create an account?

Click on "Register" from the login page. Fill in your details and verify your email. You'll receive a confirmation once your account is approved.

I forgot my password. What should I do?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your email address, and we'll send you a link to reset your password.

Can I change my account information?

Yes, go to your Profile page to update your personal information, contact details, and preferences.

Technical Issues

The website isn't loading properly. What can I do?

Try clearing your browser cache and cookies, or try a different browser. If the issue persists, contact IT support.

How do I report a bug or suggest a feature?

Use the "Contact Help" page to submit bug reports or feature suggestions. Include as much detail as possible.

Policies & Rules

What items am I responsible for?

You are responsible for any damage or loss of borrowed equipment. Return items in the same condition you received them.

Can I lend equipment to others?

No, equipment must only be used by the person who borrowed it. Sharing borrowed items violates our policy.

What if equipment is damaged when I borrow it?

Report any pre-existing damage immediately when picking up equipment. Take photos if possible to protect yourself.
